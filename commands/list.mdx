---
title: "reseed list"
description: "List all skills and packs in your library"
---

```bash
reseed list
```

Lists all skills and packs in your library. Standalone skills are shown first, followed by packs with their contained skills indented.

## Example output

```
Skills:
  code-review
  python-typing

Packs:
  frontend
    accessibility
    css-reset
    frontend-design
```
